Hello

I am a Masters student at the Latinamerica Institute student tutor at the Energieseminar at TU Berlin.

I am currently interested in the political ecology of hydropower and oil extractivism in Latin America. Everything in the intersection of climate change and (mixed methods) social sciences fascinates me. My tutoring is focused on the nexus Renewable Energies - Enviroment - Society and ranged from theoretical projects on Hydropower and Solarpunk utopias to a practical construction project at Tempelhofer Feld.

If you want to talk about my tutoring, hit me up on Bluesky (@linuskb) or via mail.
